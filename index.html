The Nail Suite

HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, UX Design

Collaborating closely with a local nail salon owner, I embarked on the journey to craft the ideal website tailored to her business. Drawing on UX Design principles and engaging in continuous collaboration with the owner, I developed a format that seamlessly aligned with both her vision and her clients' expectations. In the implementation phase, I utilized HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap to bring the design to life. This project served as a pivotal opportunity for skill enhancement, particularly in the realm of site responsiveness for mobile devices. The experience not only strengthened my technical abilities but also deepened my understanding of crafting user-centric designs in collaboration with stakeholders.

Jim's Shots Photography

HTML, CSS, Bootstrap

I collaborated with a talented wildlife photographer to create a captivating online platform showcasing his exceptional work. As a passionate hobbyist seeking the perfect outlet for displaying his proudest achievements, he entrusted me to design and develop a website that seamlessly integrates user experience (UX) design principles, providing him with an ideal space to showcase his photographic masterpieces.

Ogden Valley Adaptive

UX Design, Adobe XD, Usability Testing

I was part of a team tasked to create a mobile application for a local non-profit organization, Ogden Valley Adaptive. Our primary objective was to explore and implement new design and testing methods. Throughout the project, we employed various techniques such as user profiles, paper prototypes, and usability testing to enhance the user experience. To visualize our ideas, we utilized Adobe XD for crafting wireframes and prototypes. Working alongside talented team members, Jafid Garcia and Brandon Collete, provided a valuable learning experience. Together, we navigated the intricacies of the design process, creating a mobile application that met the requirements of the assignment.
